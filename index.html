We are currently accepting donations and investments in order to begin mass production of Trinity Space Exploration Ships. We currently have 50 Ships in use, but intended to produce enough Ships for each household in the USA.

Our Mission

We explore the unexplored searching for life on other planets that have been overlooked by other agencies, while producing Ships for everybody to use. Trinity Space Exploration (TSE) utilizes innovative technologies, such as 3D printing, to create cheap space travel. Our methods allow us to be able to explore planets that may contain life for half the costs other agencies quote. TSE intends to disrupt the space travel industry and aims to dominate the market within the next 10 years, and ultimately mass producing space rockets for the average person to use.
